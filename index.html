⚖️ $DLTA (Arbitrage)

Ranks a player based on their DFS Salary and subtracts their rank based on Live Win Probability. A positive number indicates the player is underpriced by DraftKings compared to Vegas.

🚂 STEAM (wVEL)

The velocity of odds movement from Sunday open to current Tuesday lines. Highly correlated with sharp money. We track any movement > 2%.

🎯 PROFILE FIT

A 1-100 quantitative grade matching a player's recent stats (Heavily weighted to SG:APP and SG:ARG) against the historical winning profile of Harbour Town.

👥 pOWN% & LEVERAGE

Projected ownership accounts for the "Tout Bump" (inflated chalk). The Leverage score (1-100) finds players with elite Profile Fits that the public is ignoring.

⏱️ TEE TIME WAVE

AM/PM tags derived from official Thursday tee times. The engine mathematically penalizes the PM wave to account for historically higher coastal afternoon winds.

🧮 KNAPSACK OPTIMIZER

A dynamic programming algorithm that evaluates millions of combinations. It strictly forces a total spend between $49,000 and $50,000 to maximize structural ceiling.
