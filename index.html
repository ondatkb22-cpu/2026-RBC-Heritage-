Whether you're a casual fan building your first $1 DraftKings lineup or a high-stakes sharp optimizing 150 entries, the Plaid Genie levels the playing field. We turn raw data, course history, and live Vegas odds into actionable, mathematical advantages.

1 The $DLTA Metric

DraftKings salaries are set on Monday. Vegas odds move all week. $DLTA bridges this gap. We rank a player based on their DFS Salary, and subtract their rank based on Live Win Probability.


A Positive $DLTA means the player is mathematically underpriced on DraftKings relative to their current chance of winning. It is the ultimate value identifier for cash games and tournament anchors.

2 Sharp Money Steam (wVEL)

Books open their lines on Sunday night. By Wednesday, sharp bettors and syndicates have hammered the inefficiencies. Plaid Genie tracks this velocity.


The wVEL (Steam) Indicator flags players whose implied win probability has surged by more than 2% since the open. If the smart money is heavily backing a player, they earn the STEAM badge on your board.

3 12-Column Custom Course Logic

No two golf courses are the same. Our engine allows you to ingest up to 12 columns of highly specific data. We weigh generalized stats (SG: Approach, Birdie %) against course-critical metrics.


For example: On tight, short courses like Harbour Town, the engine heavily weights Proximity from 100-150 Yards and GIR from the Rough to bubble up the true course horses.

4 Automated Tiering

Stop guessing who to put in your player pool. Plaid Genie processes your imported data through our composite algorithm to automatically assign players to tiers.


The TARTAN tier highlights the absolute elites for the given course. The LIGHTHOUSE tier provides your reliable, high-floor mid-range options to complete your optimizer builds.
