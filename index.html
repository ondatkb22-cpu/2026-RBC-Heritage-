1 The $DLTA Metric

        
DraftKings salaries are set Monday. Vegas odds move all week. $DLTA bridges the gap — we rank a player by salary and subtract their rank by live win probability. A positive $DLTA means the player is mathematically underpriced.
      
      
        

2 Sharp Money Steam (wVEL)

        
Books open their lines Sunday night. By Wednesday, syndicates have hammered the inefficiencies. wVEL tracks this velocity — flagging players whose implied win probability has surged since the open.
      
      
        

3 Profile Fit Score

        
The Profile Fit score (1–100) dynamically weights SG: Approach and SG: Around-the-Green to match the historical winning profile of Harbour Town.
      
      
        

4 Dynamic Optimizer

        
The optimizer evaluates millions of roster combinations to construct the optimal 6-man lineup under the $50k cap. Lock (🔒) to force a player in, Exclude (✕) to fade.
