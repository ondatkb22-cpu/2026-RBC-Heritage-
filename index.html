1 The $DLTA Metric

DraftKings salaries are set on Monday. Vegas odds move all week. $DLTA bridges this gap. We rank a player based on their DFS Salary, and subtract their rank based on Live Win Probability. A Positive $DLTA means the player is mathematically underpriced.

2 Sharp Money Steam (wVEL)

Books open their lines on Sunday night. By Wednesday, sharp bettors and syndicates have hammered the inefficiencies. Plaid Genie tracks this velocity. The wVEL Indicator flags players whose implied win probability has surged since the open.

3 Harbour Town Course Logic

On a tight, short course like Harbour Town, the engine heavily weights Proximity from 100-150 Yards and dynamically blends results from 2025, 2024, and 2022 to mathematically surface the true course horses.
